About Sathya's Brums

Sathya's Brums is based on my hobby of collecting scaler vehicles. Scalar vehicles can simply be described as replicas of real vehicles. What is special about this than the toy vehicles is that it is designed according to the scale of the actual vehicle. For my studies I was assigned to develop a collection/ hobby/ interest website under the module web technology and that was the basis for creating this website.
